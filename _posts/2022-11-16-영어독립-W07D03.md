### Remember this expression

```text
No longer
```

The number you have dialed is no longer in service. Please try again.

I am sorry, The item you are looking for is no longer available.

No longer A
 : 더이상 A 하지 않는다.

no longer available
 : 단종, 영구 품절.

no available at the moment.
 : 일시 품절.
