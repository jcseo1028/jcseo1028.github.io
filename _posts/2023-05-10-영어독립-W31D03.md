### Remember this expression

```text
Vicious circle
(악순환)
```

People who suffer from mental disorders often go through a vicious circle of poverty and illness.

vicious circls <-> virtuous circle

vicious
 : (행동이) 사악한, 악의 있는

virtuous
 : (행동이) 도덕적인, 덕이 있는

poverty
 : 가난, 빈곤

disorder
 : (정신) 장애
