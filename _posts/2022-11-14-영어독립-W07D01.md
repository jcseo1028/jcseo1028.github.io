### Read it out loud

```
Put yourself in their shoes
```

Before being quick to judge someone for their actions, you should always try to put yourself in their shoes.

put oneself in someone's position
 : 다른 사람의 위치에서 생각해보다.

be quick to judge
 : 섣부르게 판단하다.
