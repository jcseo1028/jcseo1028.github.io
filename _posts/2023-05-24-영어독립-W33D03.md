### Remember this expression

```text
The naked eye
(맨눈)
```

These small insects are visible to the naked eye.

There are about 400 billion stars in our galaxy, but most of them are too dim to see with the naked eye.

visible
 : 눈에 보이는

insect
 : 곤충

dim
 : 희미한, 어둑한
