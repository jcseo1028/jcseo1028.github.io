### Remember this expression

```text
Look forward to ~ing
(~하기를 기대한다)
```

I look forward to talking to you soon.

I hope to talk to ye soon.

I look forward to starting classes at my new school. I look forward to making many new friends.

make friends
 : 친구를 사귀다.

semester
 : 학기
