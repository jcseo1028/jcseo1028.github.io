### Remember this expression

```text
Have/has + 과거분사
(지금까지 이어져 온 과거)
```

Have you been to Paris?

We have known each other for 7 years.

I have lost my wallet.

Jeremy has just broken up with his girlfriend.

for years
 : 수년간, 몇 해 동안

break up
 : 끝내다, 헤어지다.
