### Enrich your vocabulary

```
Dwindle
(점점 감소하다)
```

The supply of lettuce is dwindling dut to the drought.

The US trade statistics showed a significant decrease in imports from China.

lettuce
 : 상추

drought
 : 가뭄

significant
 : 아주 중요한, 의미 있는, 의미심장한

decrease
 : 감소하다, 줄다.
