### Enrich your vocabulary

```text
Stifle
(억압하다)
```

I tried to stifle my yawn during the first meeting.

Preston tried to stifle his laughter when his boss's wig fell off.

The police used violence when trying to stifle the protest.

yawn
: 하품, 하품하다

violence
 : 폭력, 폭행

wig
 : 가발

protest
 : 시위, 항의

wig fell off
 : 가발이 떨어지다
