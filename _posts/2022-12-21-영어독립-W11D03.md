### Remember this expression

```
Nothing but
```

nothing but
 : 단지 ~ 일 뿐

I want nothing but a cup of coffee.
= I don't want anything + I just want a cup of coffee
= I want nothing + but a cup of coffee

We have nothing but our lives to live.
