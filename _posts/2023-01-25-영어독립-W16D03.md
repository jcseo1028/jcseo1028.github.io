### Remember this expression

```text
Be 동사 + going to 
(~ 할 예정이다)
```

1. be going to : 미리 예정된 계획
2. will : 계획 + 화자의 의지 포함.

I'm going to call you.
I will call you.

I am going to do well on the exam.
I will do well on the exam.

do well
 : 잘 하다.
